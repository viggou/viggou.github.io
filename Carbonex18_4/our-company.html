20 YEARS OF HISTORY IN A FEW WORDS…

 Articles and Media

FAMILY BUSINESS RUN BY THE SOLER-MY BROTHERS

with experience in the charcoal market since the 1970s.

 

1992  Implantation study to find a French forest region with easy access to all European countries

 

1993  Setting-up of CARBONEX in the town of Gye-sur-Seine in Aube, Champagne-Ardenne

Commenced the manufacturing and bagging of charcoal and charcoal briquettes.

 

We are expanding…

 

2004  The creation of a second production plant of charcoal briquettes in Brazil BRACO (sourced from FSC forest) and the establishment of exportation.

 

We are innovative...

 

2008  With the internal design and development by our engineers using new technologies, the creation of a high-efficient carbonization and cogeneration project (charcoal + heat + electricity) is ready.

 

2010  The French government selects CARBONEX for the “Grenelle Environment”, tasked with producing electricity from renewable resources for the next 20 years.

 

2011  Start of construction of our unique CARBONEX process installation in Gyé sur Seine, France.

 

A new dimension begins...

 

2012  The launching of the biorefinery and the cogeneration becomes a reality, with local manufacture of high-efficient carbon and production of renewable electricity at 3.3 MW, enough electricity to power 10,000 households.

 

2013 Winner of the National Stars & Métiers 2013 Prize in the category: Global Strategy Innovation
