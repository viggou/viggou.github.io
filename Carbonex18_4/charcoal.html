THE TRACEABILITY OF THE

CHARCOAL

To ensure our customers of the quality, origin and responsibility of our charcoal, we have been members of TFT (The Forest Trust) since 2014.

TFT accompanies us in the field to reinforce the

accountability of our supply chains.

Via TFT, we undertake to provide charcoal with verified traceability, produced in decent working conditions and which does not contribute to deforestation.

THE QUALITY OF OUR PRODUCTS IS CLOSELY MONITORED

Verification of fixed carbon, moisture, volatile matter and the granulometry together with tests of ignition performance, temperature and duration of embers are carried out in laboratories.
