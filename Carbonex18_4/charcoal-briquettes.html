THE CHARCOAL BRIQUETTES ARE REUSABLE

Once your BBQ is complete, if you use a kettle grill, close the vents on the top and bottom of the grill. If your grill is open, spray water until your charcoal briquettes are off. You can then reuse the charcoal briquettes for your next BBQ.

TIPS FOR LIGHTING

For healthy and environmentally friendly lighting, we recommend using the BRAISEUR chimney starter which requires nothing more than paper to light the charcoal briquettes. Your charcoal briquettes are ready in 20 minutes without effort.  See video.

 

or

 

Place the charcoal briquettes in a pyramid shape on a small ignition bag, wait 20 minutes then spread your charcoal briquettes out in the BBQ.
